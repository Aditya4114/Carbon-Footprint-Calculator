How it works
  • All calculations on this website are based on proven metrics and specific values derived from average consumption data.
  • By providing your usage within the specified ranges, we can accurately estimate your carbon footprint.
  • The total carbon footprint displayed considers an average household size of 4-5 members and represents annual consumption.
  • This approach ensures a comprehensive and realistic view of your household's environmental impact.
  • The result will be shown in the form of kg CO2e, where 'e' stands for equivalent.
  • The outcome should not be treated as a benchmark, as it depends on individual living habits and various external factors.
  • It is advisable to enter values as suggested in the "?" for better accuracy. If you don't use something, you can skip that field.
  • Make sure the details entered should be per household and not individually
